Red Canyon Hike and Eilat Snorkeling Adventure Tour

Enjoy a Red Canyon Hike and Eilat Snorkeling Adventure with direct transfer from Ramon Airport. On this exciting day tour, you will hike the beautiful Red Canyon and snorkel in the magical, underwater world of Eilat’s coral reefs. The Red Canyon Hike and Eilat Snorkeling Adventure is the best way to experience two of Eilat’s natural wonders in one action-packed day. This tour only runs in small groups with a maximum of seven participants for a richer experience.

After pickup from Ramon airport (or your hotel), you will head north to the Red Canyon, which is part of the Eilat Mountains. The canyon is a natural, red ravine made from Nubian Sandstone. The Red Canyon offers a family-friendly trail and is a spectacular way to discover the natural beauty of the desert. Your expert guide will take you deep into the canyon for an adventure fit for travelers of any fitness level or age. After your hike, head back to Eilat to cool off and immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Eilat’s coral reefs, where you will admire over 10,000 species of fish, sea creatures, and corals. Before your guided snorkeling session, your experienced divemaster will give you an introduction on how to use the equipment and tell you more about the marine life you are about to see. After your snorkeling session, take some time to relax on the beautiful beach in Eilat and soak up the sun and the views before your transfer back to the hotel.
