Timna Park and Desert Agriculture Tour from Eilat

Upon arriving in Eilat, there is so much to explore. Discover the beautiful desert and learn about its history on a Timna Park and Desert Agriculture Tour from Eilat. The tour is available four days a week with guaranteed departure. Just north of Israel’s most southern city lies Timna Park, a stunning desert reserve with otherworldly scenery. On this one-day tour, you will have the opportunity to explore the beauty of this incredible site and more.

Timna Park has a unique flora and geology, including one of the oldest copper mines in the world. On this tour, you will learn about the history of this ancient mine, including its ties to ancient Egyptian mythology. After leaving Timna Park, you will continue on to a tour of the greenhouses on a desert kibbutz, where you will pick, taste, smell, and touch the seasonal crops. You will get an immersive introduction to high-tech desert agriculture techniques that were developed in the Arava desert to grow all kinds of vegetables and fruits. This Timna Park and Desert Agriculture Tour from Eilat provides a unique insight into the enchanting desert which covers most of Israel’s land area, yet is missed by too many visitors.

**NB. This tour is only available during the winter season from October until March**
